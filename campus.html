Campus

homehome > Campus

NAGSENVANA CAMPUS at Aurangabad is founded in 1951 by BODHISATVA BHARATRATNA DR. BABASAHEB AMBEDKAR, the great emancipator of mankind, under the Umbrella of Peoples Education Society (Mumbai), is now identified as the pioneering institution campus which symbolises a vison, a dream of the Founder, being progressively translated into meaningful reality.

PESCoPE College campus included an ambitious OPEN STADIUM having an area of 7 and half acres, with a siting capacity of Ten thousand spectators, with a SWIMING POOL, having a hydraulic capacity of 5 lakh litres, a GYMNASIUM HALL was constructed in 1981 admeasuring 34 meters x 21.5 meters, providing almost all the indoor sports activities and also having BADMINTON HALL 257.13 sq. meter. Having 400 METRE STANDARD TRACK for various Athletics field Events and for the outdoor sports i.e. Foot Ball, Cricket, Hockey etc. and we have different play field available for different sports and games with the gallery for sitting to the audience. And for cultural activity Subedar Ramji Ambedkar sabhagruh CULTURAL HALL - (RANGMANDIR) is situated in campus. Four Hostel buildings, each building comprises forty-eight (48) rooms with all modern facilities and sanitary blocks. Each hostel is proposed to accommodate about 144 hostelers in 48 rooms, craved out for the purpose, thus total capacity of 564 students is available.
