Vision

PESCPE provides academic programs in Teachers Training Education IN PHYSICAL EDUCATION through Teachers Education Institution (TEI). Our vision for the future includes adding a major in designing and delivering rigorous, graduate-level professional physical education teachers,Physical fitness Trainers and coaches. To create sound knowledge manpower with global competitiveness, morality and social sense. We aim to provide exemplary programs in these areas.

Mission

To provide the state-of-art Teachers Training and motivate student to realize their own potential. To provide an embellished academic and congenial environment to students for complete learning experience. To promote intellectual, moral and social democracy to ensure all round development of the students. To promote Teaching faculties to develop Sportsmanship and Fraternity. To provide knowledge of New Rules and Advanced Techniques.
